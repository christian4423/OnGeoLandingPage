Have a Question?

How many classes do I need for the Professional Certificate in GIS?

The certificate consists of four fully online courses that are each delivered in 7-week sessions. This means that a certificate can easily be earned in two semesters (if courses are taken one at a time) or faster (if courses are taken concurrently).

  1. Introduction to Geospatial Technology
  2. Geographic Information Systems
  3. Remote Sensing
  4. Cartography

What kind of qualifications do I need for the certificate program?

There are no qualification requirements or prerequisites for our certificate program.

Who should apply for this program?

These courses and associated certificate are ideal for: (1) professionals currently working in fields that use geospatial technologies and pursuing training opportunities, (2) professionals and post-baccalaureates looking to advance or revamp their current career path, (3) teachers pursuing continuing education hours, and (4) international students seeking an educational experience and certification from an American university. You do not need to be an MSU student or alumnus to take our professional courses.

Can I take more than one course at a time?

Yes. Many students take two courses at a time. If you would like to pursue this option, email us to determine the best sequence of courses for you.

Want to talk to someone about the courses or certificate?

Email us at onGEO@msu.edu. We can talk to you via email or give you a call. Alternatively, you can give us a call at 517.258.7991. We are happy to help you plan your entire certificate course sequence, choose the best class for you to start with, or answer your questions.

What is the cost?

Each seven-week professional course costs $775.

Do you offer financial aid?

No. Some of our past students, however, have received financial support from their employers.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. The payment plan is two (2) installments of $387.5 for one course (totaling $775). If you would like to pursue a payment plan, please contact us before you register for a course, and we will work with you to implement the plan.

Are discounts available?

Yes! We offer discounts for current and former members of the military and alumni of the MSU Department of Geography, Environment, and Spatial Sciences. We also offer a refer-a-friend discount and a group discount for four or more students from the same organization taking a course. Finally, all students receive a $200 discount off of their final course in the certificate program. Email us for details about these discounts.
